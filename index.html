Located in the middle east of the world, established in the 18th century as a small fishing village,the city grew rapidly in the early 21st century into a cosmopolitan metropolis with a focus on tourism and hospitality. Dubai is one of the world's most popular tourist destinations in the world.This can be accredited to Dubai's mix of cosmopolitan lifestyle and local culture,as well as the city's having the second most five star hotel in the world and the tallest building in the world, The Burj Khalifa,because of upcoming competition and other reasons, the tallest Creek Tower is being built.
